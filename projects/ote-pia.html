New Premium Internet Access

  • Project: New Premium Internet Access
  • Client: OTE
  • Description: Premium Internet Access is a product that extends the OTE Business Product portfolio providing a Symmetric Internet Solution. Cognity delivers in the context of the OTE project the Order to Bill and Fault to Repair solution including Order Capture with Oracle Siebel CRM, Central Order Management, Service Order Management and Technical Order Management with Oracle OSM platform and Service modeling with Oracle UIM. The solution conforms to RSDOD architecture and will integrate with various OTE existing systems, such as WFM Wrapper, Promitheas, NRI, ASAP, Super Repo through SOA platform. The solution tackles the challenges of ordering a Business Product through the processes of the Residential products, without affecting their ordering or fulfillment.
  • Technologies: Oracle OSM, Xquery, XML, Xpath, Oracle SQL Developer
